Frequently Asked Questions

How far are your hotels from the Haram in Makkah and Madinah?

Our package tiers are designed to suit different needs. Our VIP packages feature 5-star hotels located just steps away from the Haram (often within 50-100 meters). Our Economy packages also prioritize convenience, offering clean, comfortable hotels within a short, easy walking distance or a quick complimentary shuttle ride to the Haram.

Do you arrange group departures from Sargodha?

Yes! While our main flight departures are from Lahore Airport, we arrange comfortable, coordinated transport from our Sargodha office to the Lahore airport for all our group departures. Our group leader travels with you from Sargodha all the way to Makkah.

What documents are required to apply for an Umrah Visa?

To process your Umrah visa, we generally require:

  • An original passport with at least 6 months of validity.
  • A copy of your CNIC (Computerized National Identity Card).
  • Four recent passport-size photographs with a white background.
  • A Polio Vaccination certificate.

(Our team will guide you through the exact requirements based on current government regulations).

Are Ziyarat tours included in the Umrah packages?

Yes, all our Umrah packages include complete Ziyarat arrangements in both Makkah and Madinah. We provide comfortable, air-conditioned transportation and an experienced guide to take you to the historical and religious sites.

Can you help me book a holiday or honeymoon package?

Absolutely. Beyond Umrah, we specialize in customized holiday tours, honeymoons, and family vacations worldwide. We handle your flights, hotel bookings, and visa assistance so you can just pack your bags and enjoy!
